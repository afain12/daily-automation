Reusable template for replying to a warm introduction when an existing provider refers a new doctor to Lincoln Reference Laboratory. Lead with gratitude, welcome the new provider, and move to a concrete next step.
## How to use
Replace the bracketed placeholders, pick a subject line, and send as a reply-all so the referring provider sees the follow-through.
- `[Referring Dr.]` — the doctor who made the introduction
- `[New Dr.]` — the new provider being introduced
- Adjust the timeframe in the closing ask as needed
## Subject line options
- Thank you, [Referring Dr.] — and welcome, [New Dr.]
- Welcome, [New Dr.] — let's get your lab set up
## Email body
> [Referring Dr.] and [New Dr.],
>
> Thank you for the kind words, [Referring Dr.] — they genuinely mean a lot. Helping you get your lab services up and running was a pleasure from start to finish, and I'm grateful you thought of me. It's always a privilege to work with you.
>
> [New Dr.], it's a pleasure to meet you. I'm Aaron Fainshtein with Lincoln Reference Laboratory. We work closely with internal medicine practices across NY/NJ, and my goal is always to make the lab side of things effortless for you and your staff — from coordinating the lab computers and label printing to setting up a smooth, reliable draw process for your patients. [Referring Dr.] can vouch that I like to handle the heavy lifting so it's seamless from day one.
>
> To get started, I'd love to set up a quick 15–20 minute call to learn about your practice and how you'd like to run your lab workflow. From there I'll take care of portal access, requisitions, supplies, and the on-site setup so you're fully operational with minimal lift on your end.
>
> Would sometime later this week or early next week work for you, [New Dr.]? Feel free to share a couple of times that are convenient and I'll make it happen.
>
> Looking forward to working together.
>
> Warm regards,
> Aaron Fainshtein
> Lincoln Reference Laboratory
