# The opportunity
Functional medicine testing looks for the *why* behind how a client feels, not just whether a number is technically out of range. Clients increasingly want this proactive, root-cause view of their health and will pay out of pocket for it. We already run most of the building blocks through AccuReference; this reorganizes what we offer into clear, client-friendly panels and proposes new ones to round out a complete menu.

# How to frame it for clients
- **Lead with the benefit.** Name panels for what they reveal (e.g. *Metabolic & Blood Sugar Profile*), with the marker list underneath.
- **Tier it.** Offer an Essential / Comprehensive / Optimal ladder so clients self-select by budget and curiosity.
- **Bundle for value.** Price packages below the sum of their parts so the bundle is the obvious choice.

# Benchmarking vs Function, Genova, Vibrant & Mosaic
AccuReference's strength is blood-based work, so most blood panels are replicable in-house; the specialty urine/stool/array tests belong to the functional labs and are better referred out.
| Lab & signature offering | What it measures | How to build on AccuReference | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| Function Health (160+ blood biomarkers) | Heart (ApoB, Lp(a), particle size), metabolic, thyroid, hormones, nutrients, metals, liver, inflammation | ~70–80% replicable = Optimal/Executive bundle + ApoB, Lp(a), LDL particle #. Galleri & ApoE refer out | Mostly in-house |
| Genova NutrEval | Nutrients + organic acids + amino/fatty acids + toxic elements | Blood nutrients & metals = Micronutrient + Heavy Metals; organic/amino/fatty acids = send-out OAT | Partial / hybrid |
| Genova GI Effects | Stool microbiome, digestion, inflammation | In-house screen (H. pylori, celiac, calprotectin) + send-out stool | Screen in-house, refer |
| Vibrant – Nutrient Zoomer | Vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, amino/fatty acids | Closely matches Micronutrient panel | In-house |
| Vibrant – Gut Zoomer | Microbiome, pathogens, digestion (stool) | In-house screen + send-out stool | Screen in-house, refer |
| Vibrant – Hormone Zoomer | Sex & adrenal hormones, metabolites, bone turnover (urine) | Serum = Hormone + Adrenal panels; urine metabolites = send-out | Partial / hybrid |
| Vibrant – Toxin Zoomer | Heavy metals + mycotoxins + chemicals + PFAS | Metals = Heavy Metals panel; rest = send-out | Partial / hybrid |
| Mosaic – OAT / EnviroTOX | 76 urine organic acids; mold, chemicals, glyphosate | Refer out — flagship specialty add-on | Refer out |

**Positioning:** a Function-Health-style comprehensive blood panel run locally through AccuReference, with specialty functional add-ons referred to Genova / Vibrant / Mosaic.
# Important flags before launch
- **Confirm exact markers** with the AccuReference rep — especially Free/Reverse T3, ApoB, LDL particle number, Omega-3 index, MTHFR.
- **OAT & stool panels are usually send-outs** (Mosaic / Genova / Vibrant). Confirm whether AccuReference offers or refers them.
- **Reposition the cancer markers** as monitoring for symptomatic/high-risk clients under provider guidance — *not* a general screen (false positives cause anxiety and unnecessary work-ups).
- **Orders, prep, billing:** valid provider order on every panel; build fasting and cycle-timing into booking; clear cash-pay (non-insurance) language.
