> **What this is:** The reusable home for all Insight specialty referral flyers. Each specialty clones the canonical brutalist one-pager design and swaps in its own formulary. Print-ready PDF + editable HTML produced for every entry below.
## How to reuse
1. Pick the specialty page below (or clone the closest one).
2. Swap the **formulary table** (4 categories) for the target specialty.
3. Update the **header strip**, **hero headline** (`SEND [SPECIALTY]`), **doc code**, and **bottom meta line**.
4. Keep everything else identical — design, 4-step workflow, Why Insight metrics, and contact are fixed standards.
5. Confirm the formulary against the **Specialty Intelligence Platform** before printing.
## Fixed standards (do not change per-flyer)
- **Design:** Swiss-industrial brutalist — navy / hazard-coral / gold on unbleached paper. Anton display type, Space Mono telemetry labels, registration crosshairs.
- **Layout:** One letter page, flush to the bottom border, zero dead space.
- **Workflow:** Four steps — Complete the Form → E-Prescribe → We Run the PA → Telehealth & Dispense.
- **Why Insight:** 80% Copay Drop · 0 PA on Office · 1 Point of Contact.
