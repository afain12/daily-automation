## The Split
**Notion = execution engine.** Tasks, CRM, activity logs, morning reports, operational databases.
**Obsidian = thinking engine.** Meeting reflections, brainstorming, strategy synthesis, connecting ideas across projects.
---
## What stays in Notion
- Master Tasks, Provider CRM, Activity Log, Standups & Reviews
- SAIPA Providers DB, Nestmate Account Tracking
- Workspace hubs (Lincoln Lab, United IPA, Nestmate, Dock Pro)
- Meeting logistics (who, when, action items)
- LabAide project management
## What moves to Obsidian
- All brainstorming pages (IPA brainstorm, Supply line brainstorm, etc.)
- Reflections + Arina notes
- Meeting *synthesis* — not the raw notes, but what you *learned*
- Strategy documents and long-form thinking
- Cross-project pattern recognition
- Personal development and journaling
---
## Daily workflow
**In Notion (morning + throughout day):**
1. Morning Report → scan tasks by workspace
2. Provider CRM → check follow-ups
3. Log activities in Activity Log
4. Update task statuses
**In Obsidian (end of day or when thinking):**
1. Open today's daily note
2. Write meeting reflections — not action items, but *insights*
3. Link to related ideas using [[wikilinks]]
4. When a brainstorm surfaces an action → create the task in Notion
---
## The capture bridge: Notion → Obsidian
When something in your Notion workflow sparks an idea worth exploring:
1. Add a quick entry to the **Insight Capture** database below
2. During your Obsidian session, process the queue
3. Mark as transferred once it's in your vault
When Obsidian thinking produces an action:
1. Create the task in Master Tasks with the right Workspace
2. Reference the Obsidian note name in the task description

## Essential Obsidian plugins
- **Daily Notes** — one note per day, your Obsidian equivalent of the standup
- **Dataview** — query your vault like a database (e.g. show all meeting notes tagged #lincoln-lab from this week)
- **Templater** — auto-fill templates with date, workspace tags, prompts
- **Graph View** — visualize how your ideas connect across projects
- **Periodic Notes** — weekly review notes alongside daily notes
- **QuickAdd** — capture ideas fast with preset templates
