## Schema (properties)
- **Account Name** (title)
- **Account Type** (select): Hospital, Clinic, Physician Office, Research Facility, Other
- **Due Date** (date)
- **Last Contact** (date)
- **Needs Visit** (checkbox)
- **Priority** (select): Urgent, High, Medium, Low
- **Status** (status): Not Started, In Progress, Waiting, On Hold, Completed
