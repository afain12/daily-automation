> **This page is the canonical design and content standard.** All specialty-specific flyers (GI, Endo, Cardio, etc.) must conform to this rubric. Last validated: v.6.2 — Biologic Referral.
---
## Design Principles
- **No dead space.** Every section tiles flush — no gaps between the footer and the bottom border.
- **One page.** Always. If it doesn't fit, cut copy — never expand to two pages.
- **Administrative zero-burden.** The physician's office should be able to act in under 60 seconds.
- **Contact is singular.** One name. One number. No ambiguity.
---
## Required Sections (in order, top to bottom)
### 1. Header Strip
Format: `INSIGHT · SPECIALTY PHARMACY · [SPECIALTY] REFERRAL — HOW TO START · REV. [YYYY.MM]`
Right-aligned: `KEEP AT THE REFERRAL DESK`
### 2. Hero Block (navy background)
- Sub-label: `↳ [SPECIALTY] REFERRAL · HOW TO START` / `INSIGHT / [SP CODE] / [DOC NUMBER]`
- Headline Line 1 (white): `SEND [SPECIALTY]`
- Headline Line 2 (orange): `TO INSIGHT.`
- Pharmacy destination box (top right, outlined in orange): address + `Hospital Outpatient Pharmacy`
### 3. Scope Tagline
One line. Active voice. Example: *"Hand off the biologic. Insight handles the rest."*
Followed by a full-width rule.
### 4. Formulary Table
Label: `§ [SPECIALTY] SPECIALTY MEDS WE FILL / CONFIRM YOUR SCRIPT IS IN SCOPE`
Right label: `FORMULARY · CALL TO CONFIRM`
Rows: Category (mono, small caps) + Drug names (BigShoulders Bold, mid-size)
End with full-width rule.
### 5. Four Steps — The Official Workflow
Label: `§ FOUR STEPS / THE OFFICIAL WORKFLOW` / right: `FOLLOW IN ORDER`
| Step | Title | Body |
|---|---|---|
| 01 (navy) | COMPLETE THE FORM | Pharmacy Collaborative Form (CDTM). |
| 02 (orange) | E-PRESCRIBE THE SCRIPT | Send to Insight. Address printed below. |
| 03 (navy) | WE RUN THE PA | Insight handles every prior auth and copay enrollment. |
| 04 (orange) | TELEHEALTH & DISPENSE | Telehealth call with our pharmacist. No charge. |
Steps separated by vertical rules. No horizontal rule below.
### 6. Pharmacy CTA Band (navy background)
- Orange arrow `→` left accent
- Label: `PHARMACY DESTINATION · E-PRESCRIBE TO:`
- Sub: `INSIGHT HOSPITAL OUTPATIENT PHARMACY` (orange)
- Large address: `2525 S MICHIGAN AVENUE` (white) + `CHICAGO, IL` (right-aligned, gray)
### 7. Why Insight (3 columns)
Label: `§ WHY INSIGHT / VS. OTHER SPECIALTY PHARMACIES`
| Metric | Color | Label | Body |
|---|---|---|---|
| 80% | Orange | COPAY DROP | $1,800–$4,000 → $80–$200 via assistance |
| 0 | Navy | PA ON OFFICE | Insight runs every prior auth. Team stops chasing. |
| 1 | Blue | POINT OF CONTACT | One pharmacist. One televisit. Updates return to referrer. |
### 8. Footer Band (orange background) — FLUSH TO BOTTOM
⚠️ **Critical:** This band must sit directly above the bottom meta line with zero gap. There must be no dead space between the footer and the bottom border.
Three columns:
- **Left:** `↳ QUESTIONS · FORM` / `CONTACT` (white, large) / `[LIAISON NAME].` (navy)
- **Center:** `DIRECT · SPECIALTY PHARMACY LIAISON` / Name (white) / Phone (white, large)
- **Right:** `INSIGHT PHARMACY` / address / `FAX / PORTAL / EMAIL` / `[confirm w/ pharmacy]`
### 9. Bottom Meta Line
Format: `INSIGHT SPECIALTY PHARMACY · [SPECIALTY] HOW-TO-START · CONFIDENTIAL`
Right: `TEMPLATE · [SPECIALTY] REFERRAL ONE-PAGER · v.[VERSION]`
