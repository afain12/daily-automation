**Evaluation of External Billing Service and Transition Strategy**

<summary>
### Current Billing Situation
- The company currently uses an in-house billing team consisting of approximately 14 people located overseas.
- The in-house team's mistakes and mishaps have become costly, prompting consideration of alternatives.
### Evaluation Criteria for New Biller
- Need to determine what types of laboratories they have experience billing for.
- Confirm they have CPC level coders and if the owner has worked directly with Medicare.
- Assess their familiarity with Telcor, the current RCM software, as it's extensive and would require significant learning if they haven't used it before.
- Identify which insurance companies they have exceptional relationships with.
### Proposed Implementation Approach
- Begin with an auditing service where the new biller would review Telcor denials to attempt payment recoupment.
- If successful, consider a three-month crossover period where they would continuously audit the in-house team.
- Based on performance during this period, evaluate full transition potential.
- speak to the billers as to how they handle denials with laboratories. Who they have connections with if they do out-of-network billing, as well as surprise billing.
- Sucess stories with laboratory billing, notable names. New york medicaids
- If circumstances were to align start wtih small denial list
- How can you assist with prior authorization
- How do you handle improper diagnosis workflows
### Credentialing Support Requirements
- Determine if they can assist with insurance credentialing even if they don't handle billing.
- Focus on key insurers that are current pain points: Health Plus, Amerigroup, Blue Cross Blue Shield, metroplus.
- A UnitedHealthcare national contract would be ideal as it represents one of the largest current challenges.
### Action Items
- [ ] Investigate potential biller's experience with laboratory billing specifically.
- [ ] Confirm new biller's experience with Telcor RCM software.
- [ ] Determine which insurance companies the new biller has strong relationships with.
- [ ] Inquire about credentialing support capabilities for key insurers.
- [ ] Plan initial auditing service as proof of concept.
