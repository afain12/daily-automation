- Lab company handles specimen processing in-house, including separation of plasma, urine into containers, and other special handling requirements
- Custom eClinical extension allows staff to type test name and instantly see required tubes and instructions, including panel contents
- Portal access provides preliminary results while final results pending in eClinical
- Lab company provides all supplies at no charge: tubes, tourniquets, vacutainers, racks, bags, butterfly needles (23, 25 gauge with safety locks)
- Supply ordering via WhatsApp chat or email with same-day delivery, including stat drop boxes for urgent needs
- Usage tracking to prevent supply waste while remaining generous with legitimate needs

### Critical Results & Customer Support
- 15-minute critical result notification turnaround via direct communication (not just phone calls)
- Lab company principals personally available 24/7 for urgent needs, including weekend pickups
- Dedicated support team available during business hours for portal questions and service issues
- Recent example: medical director called directly about abnormal CBC indicating leukemia, immediate physician contact facilitated

### Insurance Coverage & Billing
- Clinic bills insurance directly; lab company provides fee-for-service
- Covered insurances: Full Medicaid coverage (HMO plans including Health First, Metro Plus, community plans)
- Out of network: Blue Cross commercial products, Cigna
- Willing to offer discounted cash pay rates for uninsured patients
- Lab company transparent about which insurances to avoid sending specimens to maintain in-network status

- Respiratory panel: Full viral panel billing only 5 targets at conservative $150 (not rapid tests)

- Other capabilities: CBC, lipid panels, urine drug screening, PSA, standard metabolic panels
