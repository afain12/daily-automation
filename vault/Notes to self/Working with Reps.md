When it comes to working with offering products to reps, its all about simplicity, in the fewest words possible. How are you able to achieve, define your desired output, intrigue the reps to want to sell, and most of all answer "whats in it for them".

When calculating percentages to give to reps, dont just throw numbers out there, wait, be patient let them come to you first, THEN counter plus undercut you original number by 20-30%.

PREPARE, PREPARE, PREPARE, and PREPARE. Dont just go off on a whim, have your mental strucute already established before meeting with them, anticipate thier ansers and reactions ahead of time no to be left sidewinded.
