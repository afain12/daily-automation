# Meeting Prep Checklist

Learned from the Abos/Aileen Cardio Pro meeting (Apr 14, 2026).

## Before any partner/team meeting, prep:

1. **The numbers** — percentage splits, commissions, financial breakdown. Have them written down, not in your head.
2. **The "why it matters to them" narrative** — don't assume they'll connect the dots. Explain what the business (e.g., United IPA) actually does for *them* specifically.
3. **Their likely questions** — what will they push back on? Have answers ready.
4. **The ask** — what do you need from them coming out of this meeting? Be explicit.

## Anti-pattern to avoid

Walking in with the system's perspective ("we want to roll this out") without translating it into their perspective ("here's what this means for your practice/commission/patients").

## When this applies

- IPA provider onboarding conversations
- Cardio Pro partner discussions
- Any meeting where someone needs to *buy in*, not just be informed
