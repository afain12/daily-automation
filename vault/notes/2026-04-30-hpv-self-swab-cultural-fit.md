# HPV self-swab as cultural-fit wedge

The FDA-cleared patient-administered vaginal HPV self-swab (women 30–65)
eliminates the GYN exam chair entirely. AFFA flagged this as
**particularly beneficial for South Asian and culturally sensitive
populations** where exam-chair screening is a soft refusal point.

**Sales angle:** when pitching clinics with culturally observant patient
bases (South Asian, Orthodox Jewish, Muslim, conservative Christian), the
self-swab isn't just a convenience feature — it's a screening-rate lever.
Patients who would otherwise refuse Pap follow-up can now stay current on
cervical cancer screening at home.

**Tie-in:** Kader's HPV flyer (already in Notion, edited 4/28) targets
exactly this gap. Worth packaging the self-swab + flyer + cash-pay
pricing as a single "screening-rate uplift" pitch for culturally
sensitive practices.
