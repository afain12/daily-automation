A huge painpoint for TCM is getting the patients to the office if patient is in brooklyn and available pcp is only in queens. In order to combat this, the following concept was created,
Telemedicine TCM
coded, charted, and delivered by DocPro
Using HIPPA compliant software like Doxy.me, patients are able to access thier telemedicine visit with their patients(refferal or reminder links via email notification if provided)
Instead of getting the patients medical discharge notes, being faxes or handed in a massive bundle, we are able to have everything in PDF form and parsed by Docpro software for easy reviewal and only the information that matters to standout to the office while on the telemedicine call.
